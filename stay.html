Come
& Stay

Hospitality rooted in the culture of the gift
A different kind of welcome

Not a hotel.
A living space.

The Wild Nest does not operate on the logic of the market. There are no rates, no booking engines, no star ratings. What we offer is something older and rarer: a home that opens itself to strangers who, we suspect, are not strangers at all.

This hospitality is rooted in what we call the culture of the gift — the belief that generosity, when offered without calculation, creates bonds that transactions never can. You are not a customer here. You are a guest, which is something entirely different.

"The guest arrives as a stranger and leaves as something the house will always remember."

In exchange we ask nothing material. What we ask is harder and more valuable: your genuine presence, your honest intention, and your willingness to contribute to the space — in whatever form that naturally takes.

01

What the nest offers

  • A private room in the heart of Ferrara
  • Vegan meals, cooked with care and shared together
  • Tantra practices, massage, and conscious touch — offered freely
  • Body painting sessions and creative collaboration
  • Deep conversation, silence when needed, and real human presence
  • No fixed time limit — stay as long as the journey calls for
  • A base for exploring Ferrara, the Po Delta, Ravenna, Bologna, Venice
02

What we ask of you

  • Your honesty — about who you are and what you are looking for
  • Your genuine curiosity — this is not a place for passive consumption
  • Respect for every boundary, spoken and unspoken
  • A contribution to the shared life of the space — cooking, conversation, creation
  • The willingness to be truly present
  • An open exchange before you arrive — so we can feel if our paths align

How it works

01

Check the calendar

Below you will find the availability calendar for The Wild Nest. Occupied periods are marked — no names, no details, just the dates. If your desired period is free, move to step two.

02

Send us your application

Fill in the short questionnaire below. Not a form — a conversation on paper. Tell us who you are, where you are in your life, and what draws you here. There are no right or wrong answers. We read every word.

03

We write back — personally

If something resonates, Andrea or Beatrice will reply directly. We may want to exchange a few messages, speak by voice, or video call before confirming. This conversation is not a formality — it is already part of the experience.

04

You arrive

The door is open. The nest is alive. Whatever happens next, it will be real.
